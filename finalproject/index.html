Welcome to the Library!

Welcome to Jarie Suisen's Library, a new resource for borrowing books that you will be able to enjoy.

I have gathered hundreds of stories over the years and hope to be able to share their wondrous tales with you.
This is a new library (so new, in fact, that it doesn't exist yet!), so we are still trying to get everything textbook ready, but hope to be able to provide the best of services that we can.

At this library, you can check out books, read in comfort on-site, and attend a variety of activities and events that we have during the year.

Library Cards

Obtaining a library card is done at the library and costs $5 per year. (This is a privately-owned library, so we are not supported by taxes like most public libraries are.)

Planning Events

Should you wish to schedule an event at this location, you can send an email to events@jslibrary.org and we will get back to you soon. Reservations must be made at least two weeks in advance, and are accepted on a first-come first-served basis.
